Disappearing Ice

Ice in the Arctic is disappearing. President Obama referenced this in the context of National Geographic revising their maps to reflect changes in arctic ice.

Impact on Health

These environmental changes affect people living near the arctic, and, as a result, public health. Scientific American reported on these impacts in 2014. The disappearance of ice in the arctic affects everyday life for those near the arctic. There are new dangers and previously dependable food sources are vanishing.

Future

As more ice disappears, more communities are impacted. Food source scarcity, floods and intense natural disasters will all become more common.
